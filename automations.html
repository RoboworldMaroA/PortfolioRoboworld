I am an Mechanical Engineer (Master of Science) and Software Developer (Higher Diploma in Science in Computing), I had the opportunity to put my knowledge into practice while working as an Automation Engineer in the R&D department of Prodieco. I also operated and programmed ABB robots at the Burnside Autocyl factory. Below is the design of a tablet sorting machine and the robotic projects I have worked on.

Project 1

Tablet sorting machine.

My task was to build a machine to tablet sorting device. This machine was fitted just behind the exit from the elim.

The tablet sorting device consists of eight rollers mounted on a guide.

Two DC stepper motors allow the rollers to move along the guide and thus create a gap through which tablets will pass to the other side if their size is within a set tolerance. An AC motor was used to rotate the rollers.

The device was controlled by a Mitsubishi FX5U. To create program for PLC and HMI ised MELSOFT GX Works 3 and GT-DESIGNER 3.

The algorithm for determining the drop zone was created in Excel and converted to PLC.
